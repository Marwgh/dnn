Danish Society for Nature Conservation

We work widely to create space for nature, among other things. by converting agricultural land to nature, connecting nature areas and preserving natural pearls.

We work to increase biodiversity through campaigns such as Space for the Wild and the Butterfly Effect and by working for 20 percent untouched forest in Denmark.

We inform and motivate everyone to take part in improving the conditions of nature, from private gardens through Non-toxic Garden for volunteer stewardship projects and eternity trees .
